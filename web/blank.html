Troubleshooting Tips

1. Make sure the url has "https://" or "http://" before the domain.

2. Some websites do not support iframes, such as YouTube and GitHub. It simply just won't work.

3. But as for putting google in an iframe, type in "https://google.com/webhp?igu=1".
Make sure to add the "/webhp?igu=1".
